Fast Track: Quick Answers

Skip the email if your question is common! Here’s what most students ask us right away.

Q. Can I learn while working full-time?

Absolutely! Most of our programs are designed for career switchers. We offer evening cohorts and self-paced modules to fit your schedule.

Q. Do you offer job guarantees?

We offer a Career Success Commitment. We work with you until you're hired, providing unlimited coaching and interview prep sessions.

Q. What if I'm a complete beginner?

Perfect! We start from the fundamentals. Our "Foundations" week is specifically designed to get everyone up to speed, no matter their background.

Q. Are there flexible payment plans?

Yes! From monthly installments to Income Share Agreements (ISAs) in select regions, we make sure finances aren't a barrier to your growth.
